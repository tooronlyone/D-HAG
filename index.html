Digi_TransX
AI-Powered Goods Transport Digitization System · Pakistan

Pakistan's traditional goods transport sector operates on outdated, manual processes — phone calls, paper records, human-dependent coordination. Digi_TransX replaces all of it with a unified, AI-controlled platform that handles order placement, vehicle dispatch, real-time tracking, and logistics management.
